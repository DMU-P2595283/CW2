My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia, an anime based in a hero society that follows one student in particular, Izuku Midoriya. The anime follows Izuku Midoriya on his journey to becoming a pro hero despite being born quirkless. It also follows the developme nt of the classmates within his class. The show also manages to display some serious real world struggles like mental health, through the characters and their development. For example, Shoto Todoroki not only shuts most people out but he struggles with some form of PTSD as well as having a dysfunctional family.

Attack On Titan

Attack on Titan, an anime based in the year 1893 with a setting that is heavily inspired by Germany and Madagascar, it follows three childhood friends who grow apart over the years. it focuses on the secret power one of them (Eren Yeager) has. In this world there are giant human like monsters known as 'Titans', Eren has the power to transform into one and is therefore a main character, alongside his childhood friends he fights off the titans to protect the walls. As a chikld, Yeager had a severe hatered for the titans and so made a vow that he would slay every single titan that he saw, not realising he too had the same power as them. He later realises he is not the only one of the survey corps with such a power.
